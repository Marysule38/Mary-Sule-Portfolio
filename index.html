About Me

Driven by outcomes.
Powered by people.

01

I am Mary Sule — a Project, Program and Product Manager who finds purpose at the intersection of strategy, people, and execution. I got into this work because I am genuinely fascinated by what happens when the right structure meets the right team: ideas stop being ideas and start becoming real things that real people use.

My work spans fintech, SaaS, edtech, digital transformation, and the creative industries. In every space, I have been the person who holds the thread — between engineers and executives, between ambition and delivery, between the plan on the slide deck and the product on the user's screen. I manage complexity without adding to it. I communicate across disciplines without losing nuance. I build the trust that makes fast-moving teams actually move fast.

I carry a product mindset into everything I do — thinking beyond delivery to ask whether the right thing is being built in the first place. And when I step into community work, I bring the same rigour: the programs I have contributed to have reached over two thousand people across tech, Web3, and beyond.

I work remotely and across time zones without skipping a beat. If you need someone who brings clarity where there is confusion, momentum where there is stagnation, and care where there are people — that is exactly what I do.
